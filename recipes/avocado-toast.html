Avocado Toast

Description

A quick and nutritious meal with creamy mashed avocado spread on crispy toasted bread, seasoned with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast the bread slices to your desired level of crispiness.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork and mix in the lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the avocado mixture evenly on the toasted bread slices. Enjoy!
