Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a delicious baked dish featuring layers of wide pasta sheets, savory meat or vegetable sauce, creamy cheese, and rich bechamel sauce. These layers are stacked, baked until the top is golden and bubbly, creating a warm and hearty meal that's comforting and satisfying. üçΩÔ∏è

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Ground beef or sausage (or vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bechamel sauce (made with butter, flour, and milk)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Herbs like basil and oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Cook lasagna pasta sheets according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage (or vegetables for a vegetarian version) to the pan, cook until browned. Season with salt, pepper, basil, and oregano.
  5. Pour tomato sauce or marinara sauce into the pan, simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  7. In a separate pan, make the bechamel sauce by melting butter, adding flour, and slowly whisking in milk until thickened. Season with salt and pepper.
  8. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat (or vegetable) sauce. Lay pasta sheets on top.
  9. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the pasta sheets, followed by a layer of bechamel sauce.
  10. Repeat layers of meat (or vegetable) sauce, pasta sheets, cheese mixture, and bechamel sauce until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of bechamel sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  11. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbly.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
